HiLinkup exposes models through a unified endpoint. Availability may depend on the account, selected model, and upstream provider state.

Use stable model IDs returned by `/v1/models`, set explicit timeouts, and monitor latency and error rates per model.

For production traffic, prepare a tested fallback strategy rather than changing models only after an outage.
