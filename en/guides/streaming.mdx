---
title: "Streaming responses"
description: "Receive model output incrementally."
keywords: ["streaming", "SSE", "Chat Completions"]
icon: "waves"
---

Set `stream: true` in a Chat Completions request. The response is delivered as Server-Sent Events.

```python
stream = client.chat.completions.create(
    model="qwen3.7-plus",
    messages=[{"role": "user", "content": "Explain streaming."}],
    stream=True,
)

for chunk in stream:
    print(chunk.choices[0].delta.content or "", end="")
```

Handle disconnects, timeouts, and a final empty delta in your client.
